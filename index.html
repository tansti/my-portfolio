About Me

Hey, I’m Juancho—your friendly neighborhood wild spirit with a heart full of adventure! I’m always chasing the next trail, summit, or view that’ll probably make me question my life choices. Whether I’m clumsily stumbling through lush forests, pretending I know what I’m doing while hiking up mountains (spoiler: I don’t), or diving into the unknown like it’s a reality show challenge—nature is basically my playground. I thrive on the thrill of thinking I’m totally prepared… only to realize I forgot my water bottle halfway up a mountain. If there’s a hidden path or a “shortcut” that looks a little too sketchy, I’m there. Life’s too short to stay still—especially when staying still involves chores. So let’s get lost, find some breathtaking views, and maybe, just maybe, I’ll find the trail I was supposed to be on in the first place! 🌿 🌍 🚀
